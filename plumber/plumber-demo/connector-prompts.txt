# Connector Prompts

## Connector 1 → 2: Night basement → Dawn street (Scene 1 end → Scene 2 start)
Pull up and back out of the dark basement, rise through the ground, the camera 
breaks the surface into dawn light. A residential street appears, soft morning 
fog. Glide forward toward the plumber's truck parked at the curb, tool 
compartments open, steam rising from a manhole. Seamless flowing aerial 
transition from midnight to dawn. No text, no people.

## Connector 2 → 3: Dawn truck → Basement fix (Scene 2 end → Scene 3 start)
Pull up from the truck's tailgate, rise above the roofline, cross the street 
in a smooth aerial arc. Descend through a basement window into a repair 
workspace. Land on the left side of the split — rusted old pipe. The right 
side — gleaming copper — waits just out of frame. Seamless flowing transition. 
No text, no people.

## Connector 3 → 4: Basement fix → Morning kitchen (Scene 3 end → Scene 4 start)
Pull back from the gleaming copper pipes, rise up through the ceiling of the 
basement, ascend through the house's interior floors. Emerge into a bright 
sunlit kitchen where a family enjoys breakfast. Continue rising up through 
the roof and float above the house. The plumber's truck is just pulling away 
below. Seamless flowing transition from fix to peace. No text, no people.
